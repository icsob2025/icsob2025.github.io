  <p>The <strong>Haus der Wirtschaft Baden-Württemberg</strong> is a historic congress, exhibition, and service centre for promoting small and medium-sized enterprises in the state. Located in the heart of Stuttgart, it hosts exhibitions, congresses, and events of regional and national significance year-round.</p>

  <p>Built between 1890 and 1896 by architect Skjold Neckelmann, its facades were designed in the Italian Renaissance style. Today—125 years later—the building still houses several departments of the Baden-Württemberg Ministry of Economic Affairs alongside institutions like Baden-Württemberg International, the Design Center, and the Patent and Trademark Center of the Stuttgart regional authority.</p>
